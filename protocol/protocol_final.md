Final protocol summary

Review scope:
- Peer-reviewed, application-oriented GNN studies
- Repositories: IEEE, ACM, Elsevier/ScienceDirect, SpringerLink
- Language: English
- Coverage window: 2019 through February 2026 (partial 2026)

Critical inclusion criteria:
1. Within scope years and repositories
2. Explicitly identifiable GNN family/variant
3. Application-oriented framework/model/method contribution
4. Empirical validation through implementation or experimentation

Descriptive extraction fields:
- GNN variant(s)
- Task granularity
- Toolkit / implementation stack
- Learning setting
- Graph type
- Dataset domain and availability
- Loss functions
- Embedding-representation models
- Aggregation strategies
- Pooling/readout/dropout / embedding-size reporting
- Within-study base-model comparison information

Interpretation note:
The review should be interpreted as a systematic synthesis of a peer-reviewed, application-oriented GNN corpus rather than as an exhaustive census of all GNN literature.
