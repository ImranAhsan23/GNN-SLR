This review uses a single precision-oriented core Boolean query across IEEE, ACM, Elsevier/ScienceDirect, and SpringerLink.
The exact syntax varies slightly by platform interface, but the underlying logical structure is the same:
(Graph Neural Network) AND (GNN) AND (Graph Neural Network based classification) AND (GNN based classification)

The review corpus covers studies published from 2019 through February 2026.
For presentation consistency, the manuscript refers to the period as 2019--2026, but 2026 reflects only records indexed up to the end of February 2026 and is therefore a partial publication year.
